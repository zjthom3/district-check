What’s included in the audit

This is not a generic accessibility memo. It is a district-specific package designed to help you document a real good-faith review before or after the ADA Title II deadline.

✓
Up to 15 tools reviewed. We audit the core tools your district actually uses, not just the ones already in our public lookup.
✓
Risk scorecard per tool. Every tool is rated Critical, High, Medium, or Low with the specific documentation gap that drove the rating.
✓
WCAG 2.1 AA gap analysis. We translate the vendor documentation problem into practical district risk instead of handing you a vague summary.
✓
Vendor outreach emails pre-written. Every tool with a documentation gap includes a ready-to-send VPAT request or follow-up draft.
✓
Executive summary for leadership. Your superintendent, IT director, or legal reviewer gets a short version they can actually use.
✓
Compliance documentation trail. The finished package is designed to help prove your district took accessibility seriously and acted promptly.

How it works

This is built to be fast for busy district teams. The intake is short, the audit is human-reviewed, and the output is ready to file.

1

Fill out the intake form

Submit your district name, contact details, and your current edtech stack. If your list is incomplete, send the rough version and we can help refine it.

2

Within 48 hours of receiving your intake, you get a report, a priority action plan, and vendor outreach language that helps you move immediately.
